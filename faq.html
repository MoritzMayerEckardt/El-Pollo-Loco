El Pollo Locco

FAQ

How do I win the game?
Your goal is to avoid dying and defeat the Endboss. Collect coins, exchange them for bottles, defeat enemies, and be prepared to face the Endboss to win the game!
How do I control Pepe?
You can move Pepe left and right using the arrow keys. Press the space bar to make him jump. Use the "D" key to throw bottles and the "W" key to buy a bottle using 5 of your collected coins.
How do I defeat enemies?
There are two ways to defeat enemies. You can jump on them from above or throw bottles at them. If you throw enough bottles, you can even defeat the Endboss!
What happens if Pepe gets injured?
If Pepe is hit by chickens or chicks, he gets injured and loses a life. Be careful and try to avoid the enemies or defeat them before they hit you. Make sure to defeat the Endboss before he might hit you!
How can I see Pepe's status?
There are five bars displayed above the screen:
  • Pepes Life: Shows Pepe's current health status.
  • Bottles: Indicates how many bottles Pepe has.
  • Coins: Shows how many coins Pepe has collected.
  • Endboss's Life: Displays the current health status of the Endboss.
What are coins used for?
Coins are valuable! When you collect 5 coins, you can exchange them for a bottle. These bottles can be used to throw at enemies or to defeat the Endboss.
How do I restart the game?
You can restart the game by either reloading the web page or, if available, clicking the restart button at the end of the game.
How do I enter fullscreen mode?
To enter fullscreen mode, click on the fullscreen icon and press the "Esc" key to exit fullscreen mode. It's recommended to leave fullscreen mode at the end of the game to easily restart.
How do I mute the sound?
To mute or unmute the sound, click on the audio icon. If you're in fullscreen mode, exit fullscreen mode to access the sound controls.
